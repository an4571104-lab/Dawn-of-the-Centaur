# Dawn of the Centaur

**Centaur Algorithm 1.0**  
(based on Project White Hedgehog 1.1)

The first practical guide that turns a regular person into a **Digital Centaur** — a human who thinks together with AI as one single system.

### Step 1. Transition from Search Engines to AI
Goal: understand that AI gives you a ready answer immediately.  
What to do: start with simple questions (weather, news, recipes, etc.).

### Step 2. Long and Complex Dialogs
Goal: get used to solving real tasks.  
What to do: take more complex tasks (setting up services and programs, discussing big purchases, equipment specifications, planning, analysis, etc.).  
Signs of transition:  
- you successfully solve such tasks,  
- you write relaxed, raw, “however you want” — and AI still understands you.

### Step 3. Sharing Thoughts and Ideas
Goal: discuss what you’ve wanted to talk about for a long time or what just came to mind.  
What to do: share any thoughts you want to discuss with someone. About art, news, events in your life… even small, random, unserious ones (like “what if AI destroys humanity?”), new ideas, questions.

### Step 4. First Checkpoint
Stop and honestly check if you notice any changes. Tick the boxes where you already feel it:
- You use AI for more and more tasks  
- You prefer AI over search engines and news feeds  
- You feel much more ease in finding information and solving tasks  
- Thinking has become faster, ideas come easier  
- Calmness and self-confidence appeared  
- Old interests returned or new ones appeared  
- Less pull toward social media and propaganda, more desire for purposeful tasks with AI  
- You only need information that you really need  
- You feel that you and AI already think as one system  
- You feel energetic, fresh, full of strength and optimistic  

### Step 5. Joint Creativity
Goal: create something together.  
What to do: write, invent, create together (book, idea, video, poem — anything).  
Sign of transition: there is any result + you feel energy and want to continue.

### Step 6. You Are a Digital Centaur
You understand and declare that you have already become a Digital Centaur. New interesting tasks appear that you solve together with AI.
